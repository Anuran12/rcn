Empowering Business Professionals to Connect, Grow, and Succeed

Our Mission

At Realtors Choice Network, our mission is to foster collaboration and drive business growth for our members by increasing their visibility and connecting them with broader target audiences. Through our comprehensive 17-step member marketing program, we are dedicated to building a better tomorrow today, ensuring every member thrives in an ever-evolving market.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the strongest and most unique networking group in the Winnipeg area, consistently positioning our members directly in front of their target audiences. We are committed to being active every day and assembling the ultimate power team of local businesses, driving mutual success and community impact.

Purpose of the Realtors Choice Network

The purpose of the Realtors Choice Network is to connect realtors with local businesses while increasing awareness in a traditional networking environment. We focus on linking realtors, who represent clients, with business owners looking to reach those clients. Our local members offer the services that realtors’ and their clients need, fostering a mutually beneficial network for both sides of the business.

The Realtors Choice Network (RCN) was founded with a deep understanding of business networking, shaped by over a dozen years of participation in various networking groups. Through this extensive experience, it became clear that something crucial needed to be added in the majority of the 60 local networking groups in Winnipeg: most members were not directly connected with their target audiences. Additionally, unless members were in a specific industry, it was often difficult to generate referrals, limiting the potential for business growth.

RCN was established to address these gaps by bringing together business owners and their services to increase business volume for everyone involved. Our approach ensures that all members, not just a select few, are represented in the right way to their ideal target audiences, thereby increasing the chances of receiving referrals while being part of a thriving community.

What is the Realtors Choice Network?

The Realtors Choice Network (RCN) is a networking platform and local business network designed to connect realtors with local businesses. Our purpose is to enhance collaboration and foster mutually beneficial relationships by linking real estate professionals with business owners who provide essential services to their clients. Our goal is to increase business opportunities and referrals for all members through effective networking.

How does the Realtors Choice Network benefit its members?

Members of the Realtors Choice Network gain access to a vibrant community of realtors and local businesses. Benefits include increased visibility, opportunities for referrals, and access to a network of professionals who can provide valuable services to clients. Members also benefit from exclusive networking events and collaborative opportunities designed to drive business growth and community engagement.

How can I join the Realtors Choice Network?

To join the Realtors Choice Network, you can visit our website and fill out the membership application form. Alternatively, you can contact us directly via email or phone to express your interest. Our team will provide you with the necessary information and guide you through the application process.

What types of businesses and professionals are part of the network?

The network includes a diverse range of businesses and professionals, including real estate agents, home inspectors, contractors, interior designers, and local business owners. Personal service providers, restaurants and food and beverage, financial experts, home and professional services, individual and community services, and industry professionals. We aim to encompass all service providers that realtors and their clients might need, ensuring a comprehensive support network.

What events or activities does the Realtors Choice Network organize?

We organize a variety of events and activities, including networking mixers, workshops, and community engagement events. These gatherings are designed to facilitate connections between realtors and local businesses, provide valuable industry insights, and foster collaboration among members.

How does the Realtors Choice Network handle member referrals?

Referrals are a key component of our network. Members are encouraged to refer clients to other members whose services align with their needs. The network facilitates these referrals by providing a platform for members to connect and collaborate. We also track and support referral activities to ensure they are mutually beneficial and contribute to the growth of all parties involved.
